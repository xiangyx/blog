> Afskedsreception
>
> Skoleleder Karin Børgesen går på pension. Arrangementet holdes i gymnastiksalen fredag d. 16. juni kl. 16-17. Havreholmens Skole, Havreholmen 12.

Afskedsreception = Farewell reception
- afsked = farewell 
- farvel = goodbye

Skoleleder = School principal
- leder = leader, manager

holdes = is held
- hold: to hold, to keep
- vi hold en aktivitet = we hold an activity
- In Danish, `hold` has multiple meanings, including "to hold" (as in holding an event) and "team" (as in a sports team). In this context, it means "to hold" or "to take place."

> Farewell reception
> School principal Karin Børgesen is retiring. The event will be held in the gymnasium on Friday, June 16th from 4-5 PM. Havreholmens Skole, Havreholmen 12.
