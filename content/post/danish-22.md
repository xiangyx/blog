- hyggelig – cozy
- plads – space
- by – city / town
- familie – family

- mark – field
- altan – balcony

- Der er en stor have. – There is a big garden.
- Stemningen er rolig. – The atmosphere is calm.

- Huset ser velholdt ud. – The house looks well-maintained.
- Det ser ud som om, de har det godt. – It looks like they are doing well.
- Der er ro og plads omkring huset. – There is calm and space around the house.

### Gæster – Nøgleord og korte sætninger 

**Nøgleord (Key words):**
- gæster – guests
- vært / værtinde – host / hostess
- middagsselskab – dinner party
- forklæde – apron
- komfur – stove
- gryder og pander – pots and pans
- vinglas – wine glass
- servere – to serve
- dække bord – to set the table
- ryde af – to clear the table
- vaske op – to do the dishes
- gave – gift
- blomster – flowers
- punktlighed – punctuality
- skik – custom / tradition
- sko – shoes
- formel / uformel – formal / informal

**Billede – Middagsselskab:**
- På billedet kan man se et middagsselskab. – On the picture one can see a dinner party.
- En kvinde laver mad i køkkenet. – A woman is cooking in the kitchen.
- Hun har et forklæde på. – She is wearing an apron.
- En mand serverer vin til gæsterne. – A man is serving wine to the guests.
- Gæsterne taler og har det hyggeligt. – The guests are talking and having a good time.
- Selskabet ser velklædt ud. – The company looks well-dressed.
- Det er et formelt dansk middagsselskab. – It is a formal Danish dinner party.

**Danske skikke (Danish customs):**
- Man kommer til tiden i Danmark. – One arrives on time in Denmark.
- Man tager skoene af ved døren. – One takes off shoes at the door.
- Man medbringer en gave. – One brings a gift.
- Typisk en flaske vin eller blomster. – Typically a bottle of wine or flowers.
- Man kigger ikke på sin mobil. – One does not look at one's phone.

**Din mening (Your opinion):**
- Jeg synes, gæster bør tilbyde at hjælpe. – I think guests should offer to help.
- Det er en god gestus at vaske op. – It is a good gesture to do the dishes.
- I Danmark er man meget punktlig. – In Denmark people are very punctual.
- Det er anderledes end i mit hjemland. – It is different from my home country.
- Jeg synes, det er hyggeligt at have gæster. – I think it is cozy to have guests.
- Det er rart at dele mad og kultur med andre. – It is nice to share food and culture with others.
- Jeg foretrækker uformelle besøg, fordi de er mere afslappede. – I prefer informal visits because they are more relaxed.
