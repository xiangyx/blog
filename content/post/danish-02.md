> Jeg har været glad for at synge, siden jeg var helt lille, og da jeg var 10, ville jeg gerne lære at spille et instrument. Så mine forældre købte en guitar til mig, og jeg startede også med at gå til undervisning. Og siden da har musikken været næsten hele mit liv.

at synge = to sing

helt = completely

lille = small

ville = wanted 
    - Derivative: ville gerne = would like to

lære = learn

at spille = to play (an instrument)

startede ogsa med = started also with

gå til undervisning = go to lessons

siden da = since then

næsten = almost

hele = whole, all, entire

→ I have been happy to sing since I was very small, and when I was 10, I wanted to learn to play an instrument. So my parents bought a guitar for me, and I also started to go to lessons. And since then, music has been almost my whole life.

> Ja, det går ok. Selvfølgelig er det meget forskelligt, hvad der kommer ind på min konto hver måned. Og der har også været perioder, hvor min økonomi ikke har været så god, og så har jeg selvfølgelig været nødt til at tænke mere over, hvordan jeg bruger mine penge. Men lige nu går det faktisk rigtig fint.

meget = very

forskelligt = different, varying

konto = account

måned = month

økonomi = economy, finances

nødt til = have to, must

tænke over = think about

lige nu = right now

bruge = use

penge = money

rigtig = really, very

 → Yes, it is going okay. Of course, it varies a lot what comes into my account each month. And there have also been periods where my finances have not been so good, and then I have of course had to think more about how I use my money. But right now, it is actually going really well.

> At stå på en scene foran en masse mennesker og spille min musik. For når jeg gør det, kan jeg altid mærke, at min musik betyder noget for andre. Og når folk hører min musik, og kommer hen til mig efter koncerten og siger, at de er vilde med mine sange, bliver jeg rigtig stolt og glad.

at stå = to stand

pa en scene = on a stage

foran = in front of

masse mennesker = lots of people

altid = always

mærke = feel, notice

betyder noget for andre = means something to others

kommer hen til mig = come up to me

siger = say

vilde med = crazy about, love

sange = songs

stolt = proud

→ To stand on a stage in front of lots of people and play my music. Because when I do that, I can always feel that my music means something to others. And when people hear my music and come up to me after the concert and say that they love my songs, I become really proud and happy.

> Nej, det synes jeg ikke. Men det er også kun,fordi jeg nu er i en situation, hvor jeg kan leve af min musik. Mange af mine kolleger vil helt sikkert sige, at det er et stort problem, at de ikke tjener nok til at kunne leve af at spille. Og det er hårdt. Det ved jeg alt om, for jeg har selv prøvet det.

kun = only

leve af = live off

vil helt sikkert sige = will definitely say

tjene nok = earn enough

hårdt = hard, tough

alt om = all about

→ No, I don't think so. But that is also only because I am now in a situation where I can live off my music. Many of my colleagues will definitely say that it is a big problem that they do not earn enough to be able to live by playing. And it is tough. I know all about that, because I have experienced it myself.

---

> Det føles mest naturligt, fordi det er mit modersmål. Nu skriver jeg jo selv mine sange, og det er det sprog, jeg bedst kan udtrykke mig på. Men jeg ved godt, at hvis jeg i fremtiden også skal have succes i udlandet, bliver jeg nødt til at skrive mine sange på engelsk.

mest naturligt = most natural

udstrykke mig pa = express myself in

men jeg ved godt = but I know well

fremtiden = the future
 - decomposed: frem = forward + tiden = the time

udlandet = abroad
 - decomposed: ud = out + landet = the country

bliver nødt til = will have to

→ It feels most natural, because it is my mother tongue. Now I write my own songs, and it is the language I can best express myself in. But I know well that if I am also to have success abroad in the future, I will have to write my songs in English.

forskellige = different, various
